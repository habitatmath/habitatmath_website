Chicago – Wind in Your Hair, Deep Dish in Your Heart

City Pulse

The Windy City packs 2.7 million people, 77 neighborhoods, and a skyline that flexes hard.

Sits on Lake Michigan’s edge, with 42 km of waterfront trails.

AQI usually stays under 40 except in summer smog days.

🌤 Climate & Seasons

Winters: -6–3 °C, 40+ sub-zero days, and 35 snow days yearly.

Summers: 26–30 °C, 2,500 h sunshine.

Expect gusts up to 50 km/h on windy days—title not just for fun.

🚊 Public Transit Snapshot

CTA ‘L’ trains run on 8 lines over 165 km.

Monthly pass: $75, buses cover 130+ routes.

Cycling network: 400 km, Divvy bikes at 600+ stations.

🏨 Short Stays & Getting There

Two airports—O’Hare and Midway—connect globally; O’Hare 2nd busiest in US.

Budget stay: $110–140, mid-range: $170–250.

Pizza slice: $5, diner breakfast: $12.

💰 Living Costs, QOL & Infrastructure

1-bed downtown: $2,000–2,500/mo, outer: $1,300–1,700.

Internet 300 Mbps+ at $65.

Parks and beaches boost summer life quality.

🛡 Safety, Health, Education & Paperwork

Some neighborhoods safer than others—stick to well-lit areas at night.

Strong hospital network and top universities.

Residency paperwork simple for domestic movers.

🧭 Tips for Tourists

Try the architecture boat tour.

Skip Navy Pier if you hate crowds.

Winter? Layer like an onion.

🏡 Tips for Long-Term Dwellers

Get a Ventra pass—it’s your best transit deal.

Explore each neighborhood’s food scene.

Learn to embrace, not fight, the wind.
